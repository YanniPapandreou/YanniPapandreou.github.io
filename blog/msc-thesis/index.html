My MSc Thesis

During my MSc in Statistics at Imperial I undertook a summer research project under the supervision of Dr Andrew Duncan. This project involved inference in generative models based on ODEs. In particular, an adjoint method for gradient descent in high-dimensional parameter spaces was studied.
